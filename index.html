1.1 Reflection on Industrial Visit 1 (UTM Open Day at UTM Digital)

Watch the Video:

Watch on YouTube

1. What I have gained:
"I attended the UTM Open Day held at UTM Digital, which served as my first Industrial Visit. It was a great opportunity where various companies reviewed their latest products and shared how technology is shaping the future. I gained a clear understanding of how UTM Smart services facilitate our campus life and the importance of UTM WiFi for our academic success. Moreover, I was introduced to several AI applications that are currently trending in the industry. Participating in the Q&A session was a highlight for me, as I won a UTM-branded pen for my correct answer, which really boosted my confidence."

2. Suggested Improvements:
"Since the event was at UTM Digital, it was already very tech-focused. However, I suggest that future Open Days could include a 'Tech-Walk' through the UTM Digital servers or data centers to give students a behind-the-scenes look at the infrastructure. Also, having more booth-based short hackathons or problem-solving tasks would make the industrial interaction even more exciting."

3. Reflection on Opinion, Idea, and Creativity:
"The environment at UTM Digital during the Open Day was inspiring. My reflection is that such events bridge the gap between students and the corporate world. It sparked an idea in me: what if we had a UTM Smart App feature that uses AI to guide new students during such Open Days with real-time navigation and booth information? This visit encouraged me to be more proactive in learning and to think creatively about how our university’s digital resources can be further enhanced."

1.2 Reflection for Poster: PPG Industrial Talk 1

1. What I have gained:
"While creating this poster for the PPG Industrial Talk 1, I dived deep into how a global leader uses data to drive its business. I gained detailed knowledge about Data Analytics COE, specifically how innovations like the Marine Hull Navigator predict risks and save fuel costs. I also learned about PPG's Cloud-Only infrastructure strategy, utilizing AWS and Azure to maintain a global network. Furthermore, exploring the SAP Process Flow (Procure to Pay and Order to Cash) helped me understand how an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system connects finance, logistics, and operations in real-time."

2. Suggested Improvements:
"The session provided a great overview of high-level architecture. However, to improve future learning, I suggest including a comparison study between traditional industrial methods and these new AI-driven models. Additionally, a brief session on the specific cybersecurity protocols used within the Multi-Cloud Ecosystem would provide a more holistic view of the technical safety measures involved."

3. Reflection on Opinion, Idea, and Creativity:
"This poster is a result of my effort to translate complex industrial data into a clean, visual narrative. My creative idea was to use infographics to represent the SAP modules and Global Infrastructure so that even a non-technical person could understand the flow. My key reflection is that 'ICT is a strategic business driver, not just technical support.' This assignment has motivated me to bridge the gap between academic coding and real-world industrial mastery, pushing me to think more about user-centric data visualization."
